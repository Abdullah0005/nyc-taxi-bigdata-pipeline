# NYC Yellow Taxi: Enterprise Big Data Optimization & Fare Prediction Pipeline

## Project Overview
This industrial big data project engineers an end-to-end automation pipeline to clean, analyze, and optimize fleet management and dynamic pricing strategies using New York City's yellow taxi transactional data. It processes over 10 million records using high-efficiency Data Engineering and Machine Learning frameworks[cite: 8].

## Tech Stack & Architecture
* **Data Engineering**: Polars (Lazy Evaluation Mode & Multi-threading for optimal RAM management)[cite: 8].
* **Distributed Automation & ML**: Apache Spark (PySpark SQL & MLlib Pipelines)[cite: 8].
* **Enterprise Business Intelligence**: Microsoft Power BI (Dynamic cross-filtering dashboards)[cite: 8].

## Strategic Business Impact
* **Dynamic Fleet Dispatching**: Analysis reveals that passenger volume peaks during weekday evening commuting hours (5:00 PM - 7:00 PM). Shift allocations are optimized to capture this maximum demand[cite: 8].
* **Exploiting Early Morning Anomalies**: The analysis revealed that between 4:00 AM and 5:00 AM, trip density drops to its floor, but the average fare experiences a vertical surge to its daily high of $24 due to high-tariff long-distance transit. Strategic vehicle coverage is essential during this specific window[cite: 8].
* **Automated Pricing Trust**: The Linear Regression model achieved an R-Squared Score of 0.8381, providing a robust, transparent foundation for automated customer fare estimations[cite: 8].
